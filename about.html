Our SCout Story

Founded by six USC students, SCout eases the process of securing off-campus housing for Trojans. Using our intuitive search platform, easily find hundreds of rental listings from a variety of management companies, backed by honest reviews from other students like you.

With honest reviews written by other students, we'll tell it like it is - no surprises.
